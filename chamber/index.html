Weather in Kigali City in March

Temperatures on an average day in Kigali in March The average temperature in Kigali in March for a typical day ranges from a high of 79°F (26°C) to a low of 59°F (15°C). Some would describe it as pleasantly warm, humid but cool. For comparison, the hottest month in Kigali, February, has days with highs of 81°F (27°C) and lows of 59°F (15°C). The coldest month, July has days with highs of 78°F (26°C) and lows of 55°F (13°C). This graph shows how an average day looks like in Kigali in March based on historical data.
